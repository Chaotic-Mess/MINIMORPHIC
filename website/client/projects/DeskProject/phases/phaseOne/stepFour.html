2026 Desk Project / Phase One / Step Four

Cable Management Setup

Implementing hidden cable routing and mounting the AV switches/splitter under the desk for a clean, professional appearance.

Cable Routing Strategy

  • Use desk's built-in cable channels
  • Install cable trays or conduits as needed
  • Label all cables for easy troubleshooting
  • Ensure cables have slack for height adjustment

Component Mounting

  • Mount HDMI switches under desk surface
  • Secure splitter in accessible location
  • Use adhesive mounts or cable ties
  • Ensure components are protected from dust/debris

Aesthetic Goals

Minimal visible wiring, clean desk surface, professional setup that doesn't detract from the bedroom environment.

Good cable management isn't just about looks - it ensures reliability and ease of maintenance.
