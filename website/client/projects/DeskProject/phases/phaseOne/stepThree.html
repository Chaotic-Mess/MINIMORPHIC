2026 Desk Project / Phase One / Step Three

Desk Frame Assembly

Building the physical foundation: an L-shaped electric sit/stand desk that provides ergonomic adjustability and ample space for the multi-monitor setup.

Desk Specifications

  • Shape: L-shaped corner configuration
  • Height Adjustment: Electric sit/stand mechanism
  • Surface Area: Sufficient for 3 monitors + peripherals
  • Weight Capacity: Support for monitors, PC, and accessories

Key Features

  • Stable L-shape for corner placement
  • Smooth electric height adjustment
  • Cable management channels built-in
  • Modular design for future modifications

Assembly Considerations

Follow manufacturer instructions carefully. Ensure the desk is level and stable before mounting monitors. Plan cable routing paths during assembly.

A solid foundation is essential for the clean aesthetic and reliable operation of the entire system.
