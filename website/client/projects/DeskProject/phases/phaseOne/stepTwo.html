Hardware Procurement

With the architecture finalized, it's time to acquire the necessary hardware components. This step focuses on purchasing the HDMI switches, splitter, cables, and adapters needed for the system.

Required Components

  • HDMI Switches (x3): 3-port manual HDMI switches, one for each monitor Amazon product
  • HDMI Splitter: 1-in, 4-out HDMI splitter for distributing Xbox signal Amazon product
  • HDMI Cables: High-quality HDMI cables (various lengths for clean routing) Amazon product
  • DisplayPort to HDMI Adapters (x2): For desktop DisplayPort outputs
  • USB-C Laptop Dock: With 3 HDMI outputs for laptop connectivity

Procurement Strategy

Prioritize reliable brands with good reviews for HDMI 2.0 compatibility and stable 4K@60Hz performance. Look for switches with manual buttons rather than IR remotes for reliability.

Budget Considerations

This approach should cost significantly less than a professional AV matrix while providing the same functionality. Total estimated cost: $100-200 for all switching hardware.

Quality HDMI components are crucial - cheap switches can introduce signal degradation or compatibility issues.
