2026 Desk Project / Planning Phase

Planning Phase: System Architecture & Design

Welcome to the 2026 Desk Project devlog! This is where I document the creation of an L-shaped electric sit/stand desk with advanced AV control for three monitors, Xbox, desktop, and laptop. The goal is flexible device-to-monitor routing without constant cable unplugging and monitor harrassment.

Planning Phase Overview

The planning phase focused on designing a cost-effective AV switching system using 3 HDMI switches and a splitter, avoiding expensive matrix solutions while achieving full flexibility.

Key Decisions

  • 3 independent HDMI switches (one per monitor)
  • HDMI splitter for Xbox signal distribution
  • USB-C laptop dock with multiple HDMI outputs
  • DisplayPort to HDMI adapters for desktop

System Benefits

This architecture allows any device to appear on any monitor independently, supporting gaming, work, and laptop modes without cable swapping.

Simple, affordable, and effective - the switch-based approach delivers matrix-level functionality at a fraction of the cost.
